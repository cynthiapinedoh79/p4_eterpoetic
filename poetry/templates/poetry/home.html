THE ART OF EXPRESSION

The art of expressing emotions, thoughts, sensations, and feelings is a gift. It opens before us a path of possibilities: a space to reflect, to transform. It is the art of letting a fragment of our heart escape through words, of releasing the weight that burdens our steps, of lightening the body so it may open its wings and begin a flight born from a deep, infinite inspiration.
